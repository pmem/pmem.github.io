Slack

The slack channel is a place to have an engaging discussion about all things related to persistent memory with peers and experts. We encourage you to ask questions, learn from others, share ideas and best practices, get help with technical questions, and discuss persistent memory with other developers, regardless of your skill level.

Forum

Just like our Slack channel, the persistent memory forum is a place to hang out with like-minded people. You can expect to find an active community of developers and technology experts who support and continually learn from each other. You can search the archives to find answers, ask questions, and share ideas or comments.
